The Woman

Magnetic woman
Pragmatic woman
Taking our electrons
Even if we were made of bronze

Ecstatic woman
Enigmatic woman
The light that attracts fireflies
The wonders that you surmise

A woman, the woman
Only human but somehow transcends
No need for bitter ends

Important but not impatient
Caring but not complacent
A cornflower in a graying field
How are you even real?
